about us

our story

A year of home-made experience just for family occasion that started with forgotten traditional recipe. It was when Mrs. del Mar, whose talent was honed since 1992, accidentally forgot the exact measure of ingredients but one of her children recreated a new mixture of dough, and that's how Do The Dough begun its journey.

2020 was a trial and error phase. Every occasion was a practice. Every relative served as their first set of customer's feedback until it was perfected in the first week of December 2020.

Making empanada even tightens their family bond. Together, they just dream of having a business and naming it from "making dough" to "doing it" until they came upon the official title of "Do The Dough".

With only P1,000 as their capital and having Empanada Espesyal as their main course, Do The Dough paved its way into the food industry.

Now, this small food business is making its channels outside of town as far as Lapu-lapu City (Northern Cebu), Balamban, and Tuburan down to Alegria (Southern Cebu). Do The Dough proudly presents its customers more of its best local craving products with the touch of its own signature style-sarap.

Cheers! For more doughs to make!
